# Global Smartphone Market Analysis 2024

## Executive Summary
The smartphone market in 2024 is characterized by the rapid adoption of AI-integrated features and a shift towards premium devices.

## Key Trends
1. **Generative AI Integration**: Leading manufacturers are embedding on-device LLMs to enhance user experience with real-time translation and image editing.
2. **Foldable Devices**: Foldables are moving from niche to mainstream as durability improves and prices decrease.
3. **Sustainability**: Companies are increasing the use of recycled materials and extending software support cycles.
